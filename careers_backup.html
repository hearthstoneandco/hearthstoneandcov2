Open Positions

Current Openings at Hearthstone & Co.

All roles are based in Greater Houston, TX. Remote-hybrid options noted per role.

Healthcare Recruiter

Full-Time Remote Operations

Source, screen, and place per diem and contract clinicians across Houston-area facilities. You will own your pipeline — building relationships with nurses, CNAs, and allied health professionals from first contact through placement and retention. Healthcare recruiting or staffing experience strongly preferred.

Key Skills: Sourcing, phone screening, ATS/CRM tools, credentialing familiarity, relationship management

Apply Now

Business Development Representative (SDR)

Full-Time Remote Sales

Prospect and qualify healthcare facility contacts — DONs, administrators, and practice managers — and convert cold and warm outreach into booked discovery calls for our solutions team. You are the first touchpoint between Hearthstone and the facilities we serve. Staffing industry or B2B healthcare sales background a plus.

Key Skills: Cold outreach, CRM discipline, objection handling, LinkedIn prospecting, healthcare facility familiarity

Apply Now

Clinician Coordinator

Full-Time Remote Operations

Manage day-to-day scheduling and communication between placed clinicians and client facilities. Confirm shifts, resolve coverage gaps, handle last-minute changes, and serve as the point of contact for both sides of every placement. Strong organization, communication, and grace under pressure are essential.

Key Skills: Scheduling systems, multi-party coordination, healthcare environment familiarity, conflict resolution

Apply Now

Payroll & Compliance Specialist

Full-Time Remote Finance & Compliance

Process weekly payroll for our active clinician network, ensuring accurate time entry, differential pay, and on-time disbursement. You will also support credentialing compliance — tracking license expirations, background check renewals, and OIG/SAM exclusion monitoring. Payroll software experience required; healthcare staffing payroll a strong plus.

Key Skills: Payroll processing (Gusto, ADP, or similar), compliance tracking, Excel/Google Sheets, attention to detail

Apply Now

Credentialing Coordinator

Full-Time Remote Compliance

Collect, verify, and maintain all clinician credentialing documentation — state licensure, CPR/BLS, immunization records, I-9, background checks, and facility-specific requirements. You ensure every clinician who goes on assignment is 100% compliant before their first shift. Healthcare credentialing or medical staffing background required.

Key Skills: Document management, regulatory knowledge (JCAHO, CMS), credentialing software, follow-through

Apply Now

Client Success Manager

Full-Time Remote Client Relations

Own the post-placement relationship with our facility clients. You will conduct regular check-ins, handle service escalations, surface upsell opportunities, and ensure that every facility account renews and expands. The ideal candidate has account management or client success experience in healthcare services, staffing, or a related field.

Key Skills: Account management, relationship building, healthcare operations awareness, CRM tracking, retention strategy

Apply Now

Marketing & Content Coordinator

Part-Time / Contract Remote Marketing

Create and schedule content for LinkedIn, manage our email sequences, and support lead generation campaigns targeting Houston-area healthcare facilities and clinicians. You understand the B2B and B2C staffing audience and can write compellingly for both. Healthcare or professional services content background preferred.

Key Skills: LinkedIn content, email marketing, Canva or Adobe, copywriting, CRM/automation tools
