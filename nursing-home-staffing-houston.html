SNF Staffing FAQs

How fast can you fill a last-minute shift?
Most positions can be filled within 2-4 hours. Some within 1 hour depending on availability. Our pool is active and ready to work.
What if our facility needs recurring shifts?
We can assign the same clinician to regular shifts, creating continuity and reducing onboarding time. Many facilities have dedicated staff.
Do you handle compliance and OIG SAM verification?
Yes. We verify all clinicians against OIG SAM monthly and maintain all documentation. You focus on patient care; we handle compliance.
What specialties do your clinicians have?
RNs, LVNs, and CNAs with expertise in wound care, dementia care, post-acute care, and complex medical needs common in SNFs.
Can I request the same clinician for future shifts?
Yes. If you have a great experience, we can schedule that clinician for your recurring shifts.
How do I request staffing?
Online portal, phone, email, or SMS. Whatever's easiest for you. We respond within 1 hour.
