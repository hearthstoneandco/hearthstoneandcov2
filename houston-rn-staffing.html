Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start working as an RN with Hearthstone?
After submitting your application, we can verify your credentials within 3-5 business days. Once verified, you can start accepting shifts immediately. Many RNs work their first shift within 1-2 weeks of applying.
What's the hourly rate for RNs in Houston?
RN rates in Houston typically range from $45-65/hour depending on specialty, shift type (day/night/weekend), and facility. Behavioral health and ICU specialists often earn premium rates. Your recruiter will discuss competitive rates for your specialty.
Do I have to accept every shift offered?
No. You control your schedule completely. You'll see available shifts that match your preferences and can accept only the ones that work for you. There are no penalties for declining shifts.
What if I want to transition to permanent employment?
Many RNs start with per diem and transition to contract or permanent roles. We can help facilitate permanent placement conversations with facilities you work with regularly.
Does Hearthstone provide health insurance?
Yes. As a W-2 employee, you have access to comprehensive health insurance, 401(k) retirement plans, and paid time off. Benefits details are reviewed during onboarding.
What certifications do I need as an RN?
You'll need a current RN license in Texas and a clear background check. Additional certifications like BLS/ACLS, specialty certifications (CEN, CCRN, etc.), and current immunizations depending on the facility.
