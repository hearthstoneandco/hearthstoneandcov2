Registered Nurse Credentials & Qualifications

At our RN staffing agency in Houston, we maintain rigorous credential standards. Every registered nurse meets:

  • Current Texas RN license (verified and active)
  • OIG/SAM screening and clearance
  • Comprehensive background check
  • Vaccination requirements per facility policy
  • CPR certification and specialty training (as needed)
  • References and work history verification
All RNs are W-2 employees of Hearthstone & Co.: Our nurses are our direct employees, not independent contractors. This ensures accountability, compliance, and consistency across every placement. You get professional, reliable staffing you can count on.
