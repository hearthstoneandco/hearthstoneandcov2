Privacy Policy

How Hearthstone & Co. collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Hearthstone & Co. Workforce Solutions LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who visit our website (hearthstoneandco.com), submit staffing requests, apply for clinical positions, or otherwise interact with our services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information.

By using our website or submitting information to us, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you provide when you: submit a staffing request form; apply to join our clinician network; contact us through our website; or otherwise communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, professional credentials, employment history, and other information relevant to healthcare staffing.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and the time and date of your visit. We use this information to analyze website performance and improve our services.

Healthcare and Employment Information

For clinicians applying to join our network, we collect professional and credentialing information including licensure details, certifications, employment history, references, and background screening results. This information is collected for the purpose of verifying your qualifications and facilitating placement with client healthcare facilities.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: fulfill staffing requests from healthcare facilities; evaluate and process clinician applications; verify professional credentials and comply with applicable credentialing standards; communicate with you about our services, your application, or your placements; comply with applicable federal and state laws and regulations; and improve our website and services.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for targeted advertising.

4. Information Sharing

We share your information only as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. This includes sharing clinician information with client healthcare facilities for credentialing and placement purposes (with your knowledge and consent as part of the staffing process); sharing with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business (background screening vendors, payroll processors, credentialing verification services) under confidentiality agreements; and disclosing information when required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental authority.

We do not share your personal health information with client facilities beyond what is necessary for credentialing and placement. We comply with all applicable provisions of HIPAA and HITECH to the extent they apply to our operations.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

In the event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and applicable regulatory authorities as required by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, comply with applicable legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Clinician credentialing records are retained for a minimum of three years following the last placement, consistent with applicable staffing industry and regulatory standards.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including the right to: access the personal information we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate information; request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements); opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information below. We will respond to verifiable requests within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on our website with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
